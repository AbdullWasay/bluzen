The Problem

Clear Water Isn’t Always Safe.

Contaminated water often looks clean, but can contain harmful bacteria, viruses, and impurities.

Unsafe water is linked to serious health problems:

Diarrhea (Shuban)

The most common illness caused by contaminated drinking water.

Typhoid (Tilfow)

A bacterial infection that causes long fever, weakness and stomach problems.

Cholera

A severe waterborne disease that can cause dehydration and be life-threatening.

Other Possible Issues

  • Skin problems
  • Stomach pain
  • Nausea & vomiting
  • Weak immunity
  • Poor growth in children

These illnesses are not always caused by food — your drinking water could be the source.
